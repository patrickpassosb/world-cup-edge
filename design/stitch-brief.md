# Stitch Brief

## Product

World Cup Edge: a read-only consensus-gap monitor for prediction-market traders. It compares TxLINE's cryptographically anchored sports data probability against Polymarket's live top-of-book quote for the same outcome.

## Screen

One page. No navigation. No login. No wallet.

## Data displayed

- Match: England vs Argentina, July 15, 2026, 19:00 UTC
- TxLINE Consensus Probability for England winning in regulation time
- Polymarket YES Best Ask for England YES
- Gross Consensus Gap (difference in percentage points)
- Gap After Fee (gap minus venue fee)
- Source freshness for TxLINE and Polymarket (separate indicators)
- Input verification checks (teams, date, rules, market state)
- Session alert history (alerts generated this browser session)

## Generate three directions

1. **Editorial Light**: warm off-white background, charcoal text, one cobalt accent for the gap value. Newspaper sports-section feel.
2. **Analyst Dark**: deep navy background, restrained amber accent, high legibility. Trading terminal feel without crypto aesthetics.
3. **Broadcast Data Desk**: cream and navy palette, score-bug clarity, measured red accent for alerts. Sports broadcast graphics feel.

## Prioritize

- Match identity and countdown at the top
- One dominant consensus-gap result as the focal point
- TxLINE and Polymarket values side by side, not stacked cards
- Separate source freshness indicators (not a single "connected" badge)
- Plain-language explanation beside any alert
- All seven states: loading, live, stale, no-alert, alert, unavailable, error
- Desktop (1280px), tablet (768px), and mobile (375px) layouts
- WCAG AA contrast and non-color status cues

## Avoid

- Casino or gambling styling
- Neon green or crypto-purple gradients
- Glowing cards or borders
- Card-grid clutter
- AI chat panels or reasoning streams
- Fake charts or sparklines
- Red/green as the only status signal
- Claims of guaranteed profit, arbitrage, or verified truth
- Wallet connection UI
- "AI agent" branding
